FAQ

What is Robotics‑as‑a‑Service (RaaS)?

RaaS provides robots via a monthly subscription covering hardware, software, and service—so you can adopt automation as an operating expense.

How long does a pilot take?

Most pilots start in 4–8 weeks from site survey, with measurable KPIs in the first 30–60 days of operation.

How do you integrate with our WMS/ERP?

We support standard APIs and connectors. Many workflows launch without deep IT changes using cart, tote, or pallet missions.

Are the robots compliant and safe?

AMRs include multilayer sensing (lidar, 3D cameras) and comply with applicable safety standards. Site risk assessments are part of deployment.

What service and support is included?

Monitoring, remote diagnostics, software updates, and options for on‑site response backed by uptime SLAs.

Can we scale robots up or down?

Yes. RaaS terms allow adding or removing AMRs based on volume and seasonality.
